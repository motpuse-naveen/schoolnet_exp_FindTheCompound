Instructions

1.

At the left, there are metal cylinders of various sizes. You have to arrange them in descending order of their TEMPERATURE.

To find the temperature, drag and hold the thermometer over the cylinder.

To re-arrange the order of cylinders, just drag cylinder from it's current position and drop it in the desired position.

Press the Ok button when you are finished.

2.

To help you, you are given two objects -

Thermometer

A Scratch Pad

3.

Suppose you start with the Cylinder D. Drag and hold the thermometer over it, to measure temperature.

Now note its temperature (63 in case of this example)

4.

You can use the scratch pad, to enter your reading, like this -

To write on the Scratch Pad, set the cursor on the scratch pad, and begin typing.

There is no perticular format to enter information on the Scratch Pad. Write in a way you will understand.

5.

Remember, the scratch pad is just a convenience given to you. You need not use it at all if you so choose. Instead, you can use your memory to remember the temperature readings, or simply use a paper and pencil to record your observations.

6.

After measuring the temperatures of all the cylinders, your Scratch Pad may look like this -

Depending upon your readings, you may have to move a cylinder to another position, like this -

7.

To change the position of the cylinder, you need to first drag it out from its existing position...

8.

... and drop it at the desired position.

This is how the re-arranged cylinders will look now -

9.

Using a series of such moves, and looking at the Scratch Pad, you need to re-arrange the cylinders in the descending order of temperature.

The final answer, in case of this example, will look like this -

10.

 

Press the Ok button, after you have finished.

11.

Once you have pressed the Ok button your answer will be evaluated.

You can either get it CORRECT

✔

or get it WRONG

✖
12.

If you get it CORRECT, the exercise is over, and you can start a new one by clicking the Reset button.

If you get it WRONG, you can continue to move the cylinders around and re-submit using the Ok button.

13.

At any time, you can click the Answer button. This will show you the basic movements that you have to do, to get the answer.

Showing the basic movements takes a bit of time. Once you have pressed the Answer button, a Speed button takes its place. Clicking it will show you the answer very fast.

14.

At any time you can click the Reset button. This will start a new exercise for you.
